In multiplayer games, its common to have set responsibilities for each player.  These can be divided up based interactions between enemies and allies. Note that primary enemy to enemy interactions does not exist.
# Roles
## Controller
Controllers are primarily responsible for **Enemy to Ally** interactions.
Examples include: **Tank, Crowd Controller, Debuffer**
## Supports
Supports are primarily responsible for **Ally to Ally** interactions. 
Examples include: **Healer, Support, Buffer**
## Damage Dealers
Damage Dealers are primarily responsible for **Ally to Enemy** interactions.
